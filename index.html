About

MarkerAtlas aggregates validated single-cell marker genes from CellMarker 2.0 and PanglaoDB, cross-referenced with primary literature. Confidence scores reflect cross-source agreement and tissue specificity. All marker associations link to verifiable PubMed sources.

Data sources
MarkerAtlas is a reference tool for research use only. Marker gene associations are curated from published databases and literature but may not reflect all biological contexts, species variants, or disease states. Cell type classifications are based on consensus annotations and may differ from study-specific definitions. Users should verify marker suitability for their specific tissue, species, and experimental conditions before applying annotations. This tool is not intended for clinical or diagnostic use.

Design inspired by Wassily Kandinsky's Composition VIII (1923) — the geometric precision of circles, lines, and colour fields finding order within complexity. Single-cell sequencing resolves biological identity from high-dimensional chaos; Kandinsky resolved visual meaning from the same starting condition.
