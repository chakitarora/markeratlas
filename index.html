Data sources
MarkerAtlas is a research reference tool. Marker gene associations are curated from published databases and literature but may not reflect all biological contexts, species variants, or disease states. Cell type classifications are based on consensus annotations and may differ from study-specific definitions. Users should verify marker suitability for their specific tissue, species, and experimental conditions before applying annotations. Not intended for clinical or diagnostic use.
