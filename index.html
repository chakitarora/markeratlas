About

MarkerAtlas aggregates validated single-cell marker genes from CellMarker 2.0 and PanglaoDB, cross-referenced with primary literature. Confidence scores reflect cross-source agreement and tissue specificity. Every association links to verifiable PubMed sources.

Data sources
MarkerAtlas is a research reference tool. Marker gene associations are curated from published databases and literature but may not reflect all biological contexts, species variants, or disease states. Cell type classifications are based on consensus annotations and may differ from study-specific definitions. Users should verify marker suitability for their specific tissue, species, and experimental conditions before applying annotations in published work. This tool is not intended for clinical, diagnostic, or patient care use.
